Whot is a classic card game. It is the most popular card game in Nigeria.

A Whot card suite consists of 54 cards with 6 suits: Ball, Angle, Carpet, Cross, Star and Whot.

How To Play

The aim of the game is to play your cards until you have no card at hand. You can only play a card if it has the same number or suit as the last played card. A card can also be played if it is a Whot card. If a playable card cannot be found, you have to go to the market.

There are some special cards denoted by their numbers:

1 - Hold on

When a card with this number is played, the player gets another turn

2 - Pick two

When a card with this number is played, the opponent has to pick two cards from the market.

5 - Pick three

When a card with this number is played, the opponent has to pick three cards from the market.

8 - Suspension

When a card with this number is played, the opponent lose a turn.

14 - General market

When a card with this number is played, the opponent must pick a card from the market.

20 - Name a suit

This number can only be found in the Whot suit. When this card is played, The player has to name the next suit that can be played.

How To Play With This Application

On launching the application, enter your preferred number of games to play and select your game mode. Then proceed.

To play, click on any card of your choice in the User Cards Area. If you can't find a playable card, click on the Market cards pile. Continue in this way until a winner is found.
When the Next Move Label displays Pick Two, Pick Three or General Market, you would have to click on the Market Cards Pile to continue.
